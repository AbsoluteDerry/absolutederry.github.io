december 30, 2025 · posted days ago · mood: numb

sometimes existing is enough.
not everything needs a reason.

there are days when nothing hurts, but nothing feels alive either. the silence doesn’t scream — it just stays.

i don’t call this sadness.
it’s closer to neutrality, stretched too far.

maybe consciousness itself is the sickness.

i write these things not to be understood, but to remain traceable.
